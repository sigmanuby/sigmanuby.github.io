About Our Chapter

A Legacy of Honor
& Achievement

Sigma Nu Fraternity places a strong emphasis on the concept of honor, and its members are expected to uphold the highest standards of integrity and personal conduct. The fraternity's motto — Love, Truth, and Honor — reflects the importance placed on these values, and its members strive to embody these principles in all aspects of their lives.

We uphold a strong track record of academic and athletic achievement. In recent years, the fraternity has consistently had one of the highest grade point averages on campus, and many of its members have gone on to distinguished careers in a wide variety of fields.

Recruitment

Fall Rush

We place a strong emphasis on the recruitment process, as it is an opportunity to find men who embody the values and ideals of the organization. We look for men who are academically driven, leaders on campus, and have a strong sense of personal integrity.

By hosting events and activities during the rush period, we aim to give potential new members a chance to get to know the fraternity and its members. The goal is to find candidates who are not only academically successful and involved in their communities, but also individuals who share the fraternity's commitment to personal and moral development.

We conduct our rush process exclusively in the fall semester due to the extensive activation process that follows — transitioning a new member from a pledge to a full member through a period of education and training on the values, history, and expectations of the fraternity.

1

Meet the Brothers

Attend rush events during fall orientation to get to know the chapter and see if Sigma Nu is the right fit for you.

2

Receive a Bid

After mutual evaluation, qualified candidates receive a formal invitation to join the fraternity.

3

Activation Process

New members undergo an educational process covering Sigma Nu's values, history, and expectations as they prepare for full membership.

4

Become a Knight

Upon completion, new members are activated as full brothers of the Beta Upsilon chapter.
